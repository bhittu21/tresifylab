FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can you start?

Typically, we can begin your project within 1-2 weeks of signing the agreement. Exact timelines depend on project scope and our current availability.

Do you work with businesses outside Bangladesh?

Yes! We're a remote-first team and work with clients globally. Most of our communication happens over email, Slack, and video calls.

What information do you need to provide a quote?

To give you an accurate quote, we need to understand your project goals, the features you need, your timeline, and your budget range. The more detail you provide, the better.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes, we offer monthly maintenance and optimization packages for completed projects. This includes security updates, performance monitoring, and ongoing improvements.

What's the typical project duration?

Simple projects take 4-6 weeks, business websites 8-12 weeks, and complex web applications 12+ weeks. We'll give you a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
