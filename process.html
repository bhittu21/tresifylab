Our Process

A clear, structured approach that delivers results. No surprises, no jargon—just a proven system that works.

01

Discovery & Strategy

We start by understanding your business inside and out. This isn't a quick call—it's a deep dive into your market, competitors, goals, and customers. We ask the right questions to uncover opportunities you might be missing.

What happens in this phase:

Duration 1-2 Weeks
Deliverables Strategy Document, Site Map
Your involvement 2-3 Strategy Calls
02

Design & Planning

Based on our strategy, we create detailed wireframes and designs. Every element is planned with conversion in mind. We don't just make things look good—we make them work for your business goals.

What happens in this phase:

Duration 2-3 Weeks
Deliverables Wireframes, Design Mockups, UX Flow
Your involvement Design Review Sessions
03

Development & Build

This is where your project comes to life. We build using clean, efficient code with proper documentation. Regular updates keep you informed. We test continuously—performance, security, functionality—before anything goes live.

What happens in this phase:

Duration 4-8 Weeks
Deliverables Development Site, Code Repository
Your involvement Weekly Progress Updates
04

Launch & Optimization

We don't just launch and leave. We monitor your site's performance, track conversions, and make ongoing improvements. Your website is a living asset that should keep getting better over time.

What happens in this phase:

Duration Ongoing
Deliverables Analytics Dashboard, Monthly Reports
Your involvement Monthly Review Calls
